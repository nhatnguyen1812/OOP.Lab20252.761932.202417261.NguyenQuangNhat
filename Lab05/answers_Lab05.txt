Question 1 (Section 1.3, Page 5): Compare Swing and AWT elements
------------------------------------------------------------------------

1. Top-level containers in Swing and AWT:
   - Trong AWT: Cac top-level container la Frame, Dialog, Applet. Chung la cac
     heavyweight component (thanh phan nang), nghia la chung phu thuoc truc tiep
     va ho tro boi cac cua so giao dien goc (native peers) cua he dieu hanh.
   - Trong Swing: Cac top-level container la JFrame, JDialog, JApplet. Chung cung
     la heavyweight nhung chua mot content-pane (thuong la kieu Container).
     Cac Swing JComponent khac phai duoc add vao content-pane nay chu khong
     the add truc tiep len JFrame nhu AWT. Vi du:
     + AWT: add(button);
     + Swing: getContentPane().add(button);

2. Class name of components:
   - Giao dien AWT: Ten cac lop giao dien khong co tien to.
     Vi du: Label, Button, TextField, Panel, Frame.
   - Giao dien Swing: Ten cac lop giao dien luon bat dau bang chu cai 'J'.
     Vi du: JLabel, JButton, JTextField, JPanel, JFrame.
   - Swing JComponents la cac lightweight component (thanh phan nhe), duoc
     ve bang code Java tren moi truong do hoa cua JVM nen chay muot ma, doc lap
     nen tang va ho tro look-and-feel tuy bien cao hon AWT.

Question 2 (Section 14, Page 33): What happens when you don't update for them to catch?
------------------------------------------------------------------------

- Trong Java, PlayerException la mot Checked Exception (do ke thua truc tiep
  tu java.lang.Exception).
- Khi mot phuong thuc khac goi ham play() (co chu ky khai bao "throws PlayerException"),
  trinh bien dich Java se bat buoc phuong thuc goi do phai thuc hien mot trong
  hai viec sau:
  1. Bat ngoai le do bang khoi lenh try-catch de xu ly chu dong.
  2. Khai bao tiep "throws PlayerException" o chu ky phuong thuc cua minh de
     chuyen tiep (delegate) loi cho cap tren xu ly.
- Neu phuong thuc goi khong thuc hien ca 2 viec tren, trinh bien dich Java se
  bao loi dong thoi khong cho phep build ung dung (Compile-time Error):
  "unreported exception PlayerException; must be caught or declared to be thrown".
- Dieu nay giup chuong trinh tro nen an toan va dam bao cac tinh huong loi luon
  duoc quan ly mot cach chu dong.
